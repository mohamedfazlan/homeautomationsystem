Methodology Used for Smart Home Automation System

We used the Agile methodology to develop our smart home automation system. Agile helps us work in small steps, allowing us to create, test, and improve our project regularly. Instead of waiting until the end to see the final product, we can see progress at each stage. This approach lets us quickly fix problems and make changes based on feedback. By working together in short cycles, our team stays flexible and can respond to new ideas or challenges easily. Agile helps us ensure that our home automation system is user-friendly and meets the project's goals.
